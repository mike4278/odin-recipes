Grilled Cheese

Description:

Simple and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  1. Prepare (2) slices of bread for each (1) desired sandwich
  2. Butter the top side of both pieces bread
  3. Place bread on frying pan, butter side down
  4. Place a slice of cheese (or more than 1 if desired) on top of the bottom bread
  5. Place 2nd piece of bread on top, butter side up
  6. Check progress of bottom side occasionally, flip when desired grill level has been reached
